Experienced Builder

Maia Projects is a family owned and fast growing construction company based in the Waikato but servicing the North Island. We really value our employees and want to engage people that are here for the long term, we take care of our team and we believe they take are of our business. Our company culture is the key to our delivering consistently superior construction projects, our motto in life as well as business is "let the work do the talking" and our aim is that each client is happy to refer us to family and friends.

About this role

We are looking for a self-driven, experienced carpenter who is focused on quality craftsmanship and positive work environment.

  • Enjoy a work place because our customers appreciate our attention to detail and our desire to go the extra mile.
  • Your exposure to a “wide variety” of interesting and challenging work will develop your skills and experience, as well as your future earning potential.
  • Enjoy a supportive working environment where we value the contribution of our people and are committed to seeing you develop to your full potential.

Skills and experience

  • 4 years Carpentry experience
  • Own tools
  • Current practicing license
  • Willing to travel initially to Auckland and Waikato surrounds
  • Clean drivers’ license
